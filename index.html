Predicting Grocery Trips

A case study in Philadelphia

This scenario planning tool allows you to select a Census tract in Philadelphia and forecast—using machine learning—changes to the number of annual shopping trips given the addition of a new local supermarket. Below, select to add a new supermarket of either 10, 20, or 30 thousand square feet and then click on a neighborhood on the map. The map will then show you where new shopping trips are coming from and how many (color and size). The right panel provides more precise information.
