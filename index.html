Predicting Grocery Trips

A case study in Philadelphia

This scenario planning tool allows you to select a Census block group in Philadelphia and project—using machine learning—changes to the number of visits to it given the addition of 10, 20, or 30 thousand square feet of new supermarket. Select a supply value, then click on a block group. The map will then show you where visitors are coming from (points) and how many (color and size).
